About Professor Bhattacharya

Prof. Bhargab B. Bhattacharya stands as one of India's most distinguished computer scientists and engineers, with an illustrious career spanning over three decades at the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata. His groundbreaking research has fundamentally shaped multiple domains of computer science and engineering.


As a Professor in the Advanced Computing and Microelectronics Unit at ISI, he has pioneered revolutionary approaches in VLSI design, digital microfluidic biochips, and electronic design automation. His work bridges theoretical computer science with practical engineering solutions, creating technologies that impact millions of lives worldwide.

Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata — Where excellence began

Guiding Philosophy

"Research should not just advance knowledge, but should solve real problems that matter to society. The true measure of our work is not in the complexity of our algorithms, but in the simplicity and elegance with which they address human needs."— Prof. Bhargab B. Bhattacharya

The Mentor's Legacy

Throughout his distinguished career, Professor Bhattacharya has mentored numerous students who have gone on to become leaders in academia and industry. His teaching philosophy emphasizes not just technical competence, but also the development of critical thinking, ethical reasoning, and collaborative skills.


His influence extends far beyond the classroom and laboratory, as he has served on editorial boards, program committees, and advisory panels, helping to shape the direction of computer science research and education globally.
